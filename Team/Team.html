About

The Los Angeles Lakers are an American professional basketball team based in Los Angeles. The Lakers compete in the National Basketball Association (NBA), as a member of the league's Western Conference Pacific Division. The Lakers are one of the most successful teams in the history of the NBA, and have won 17 NBA championships, tied for the most along with the Boston Celtics.
